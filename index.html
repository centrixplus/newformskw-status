Reported Gaps — Status

Item Status Pending from Ordable Details
Override Inventory Items
 Done No Orders force-placed via POS despite no stock are now clearly marked and visible in the system (channel + override indicator), on the order, the delivery note, and the daily order lists.
Order Cancellation — Stock Return
 Done No By design. Ordable remains the single master for stock levels. When an order is cancelled, Ordable's own inventory count is what governs availability, and Odoo's automatic 15-minute sync picks up the corrected number from there.
Invoice Remarks & Adjustments
Name language, contact number, remarks, address
 Pending No Display of these fields on the order/invoice is delivered. Found during review: if a customer's name/phone is corrected in Ordable after the order was placed, Odoo does not currently pick up the correction automatically. Under investigation — fix will either extend our existing 15-minute re-sync to also refresh these fields, or add the ability to correct them directly in Odoo, or both.
Invoice Design & Paper Usage
 Pending No Ticketed. Reducing product image size and line spacing so meaningfully more items fit per printed page.
"Preparing" Status in Odoo
 Pending Yes Ordable's API does not currently expose this status (only Received / Out For Delivery / Complete / Cancelled / Refunded can be read or set). Will be enabled as soon as Ordable makes it available. In the meantime, we're considering an interim local label in Odoo only (never sent to Ordable) to approximate it.

Platform Customizations Delivered

  • Sales
  • Automatic order import from Ordable — creates the confirmed order, invoice, and payment
  • Two-way order status sync: statuses pushed to Ordable; delivery dates/status pulled and refreshed every 15 minutes
  • Today's / Tomorrow's Orders views, organized by fulfillment date, with delivery type, country, area, and channel columns
  • Add-on items (e.g. an accessory bundled with a main product) correctly billed and tracked against their own product
  • Exchange / special-order orders clearly tagged, shown as a column and as a banner on the printed invoice
  • Invoice redesigned into a full delivery note — order number, tracking number, delivery slot, payment method, order details
  • Barcode & Inventory
  • Barcode scanning app shows live available stock while scanning
  • Barcode app's Delivery view shows today's Ordable orders
  • Per-variant barcodes and per-variant photos — each size/color shows its own real photo, matching Ordable
  • Full audit log of every stock and status change from each sync run
  • Sync & Automation
  • Live inventory sync confirmed working — stock levels refresh automatically from Ordable every 15 minutes
  • Nightly full catalogue sync — new products, prices, and barcodes
  • Order status and delivery-date sync every 15 minutes
  • Product photo sync
  • All sync jobs run safely in sequence and automatically retry through temporary technical hiccups, so no order or stock update is ever silently lost
